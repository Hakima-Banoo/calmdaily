Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025  ·  Effective immediately

Your privacy matters deeply to us. CalmDaily is a wellness app — we handle your personal data with care, collect only what is necessary, and never sell your information to anyone.

1. Who we are

CalmDaily ("we", "our", "us") is a wellness application that provides meditation, breathing exercises, sleep sounds, and mood tracking. We are operated as an individual business.

Contact us: support@calmdaily.app

2. Information we collect

We collect the following information when you use CalmDaily:

  • Account information: Your name and email address when you register
  • Usage data: Sessions completed, streak count, minutes meditated
  • Mood & journal entries: Only stored in your account, never read by us
  • Payment information: Processed entirely by Stripe — we never see or store your card details
  • Device information: Browser type, device type, for app performance only

3. How we use your information

We use your data exclusively to:

  • Provide and improve the CalmDaily service
  • Sync your wellness data across your devices
  • Process subscription payments via Stripe
  • Send you important account notifications and reminders you have opted into
  • Respond to your support requests

We do not use your data for advertising. We do not sell your data to any third party.

4. Data storage & security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore), which is encrypted at rest and in transit using industry-standard TLS/SSL. Firebase complies with GDPR, SOC 2, and ISO 27001 standards.

Your journal entries and mood logs are private to your account. No CalmDaily employee or system reads your personal journal entries.

5. Third-party services

CalmDaily uses the following trusted third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication and database — Privacy policy
  • Stripe: Payment processing — Privacy policy
  • Google Fonts: Typography — no personal data collected

6. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all personal data we hold about you
  • Export your journal entries at any time from Settings
  • Delete your account and all data permanently from Settings → Delete account
  • Correct any inaccurate personal information
  • Opt out of non-essential notifications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at support@calmdaily.app and we will respond within 72 hours.

7. Children's privacy

CalmDaily is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

8. Cookies

CalmDaily uses only essential session cookies required for authentication (Firebase Auth). We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels.

9. Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all your personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days. Stripe may retain payment records as required by financial regulations.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or an in-app notice. Continued use of CalmDaily after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
